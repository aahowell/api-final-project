Our Voice

Location: 44 Merrimon Ave, Asheville, NC 28801

Our VOICE, Inc. is a non-profit crisis intervention and prevention agency which serves victims of sexual violence, age 13 through adult, in Buncombe County. Learn more here.

Options Victims Assistance

Location: 412 E Meeting St, Morganton, NC 28655

Options is the domestic violence rape and sexual assault shelter, education advocacy center in Morganton, Burke County NC. Options provides empowerment services and emergency shelter to Burke County families in crisis. Learn more here.

Oasis Inc

Location: 225 Birch St, Boone, NC 28607

OASIS (Opposing Abuse with Service, Information, and Shelter) is a private, non-profit organization serving survivors of domestic violence and sexual assault in Watauga, Avery and surrounding counties. Learn more here.

Womens Resource Center

Location: 628 Summit Ave Greensboro, NC 27405

Women's Resource Center is women helping women to navigate life's hurdles, to access community services, to develop new skill sets, to move lives forward. Whatever the challenge, find the help you need - whether you are dealing with divorce, returning to work, starting a business, dealing with financial difficulties, or coping with family or health problems. Learn more here.

Cleveland County Abuse Prevention Council

Location: 407 W Warren St, Shelby, NC 28150

Located in Shelby, NC, the Cleveland County Abuse Prevention Council, Inc. provides confidential services to survivors of domestic violence, rape, and sexual assault, and homeless women and children. They do so by providing safe shelter, advocacy, support services, and preventive education. Learn more here.

Safe Alliance

Location: 601 E 5th St, Charlotte, NC 28202

Previously United Family Services, Safe Alliance assists over 12,000 people in sexual assault and domestic violence services annually throughout Mecklenburg County and Lake Norman. Learn more here.

Crossroads Sexual Assault Response and Resource Center

Location: 1206 Vaughn Rd, Burlington, NC 27217

Crossroads serves child and adult victims of sexual assault and trauma through confidential counseling, advocacy, child medical treatment, education, and community awareness. Learn more here.

Orange County Rape Crisis Center

Location: 1506 E Franklin St #302, Chapel Hill, NC 27514

The Orange County Rape Crisis Center is a non-profit agency. They serve the Chapel Hill, Carrboro, Hillsborough, and surrounding communities and provide 24-hour crisis intervention services to survivors of sexual violence. Services include 24-Hour Help Line, advocacy and accompaniment, support groups, workshops, therapy referrals and educational programs. Learn more here.

NC Coalition Against Sexual Assault

Location: 811 Spring Forest Rd, Raleigh, NC 27609

The North Carolina Coalition Against Sexual Assault is an inclusive, statewide alliance working to end sexual violence through education, advocacy, and legislation. NCCASA works on a local, state and national level to help the North Carolina community. Learn more here.

InterAct

Safelight
